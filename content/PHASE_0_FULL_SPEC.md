CHAPTER 1: How to Think Like a Programmer (10 hours)
Story Hook (Step 1 – 5 min)
“You’re at 2 AM in your hostel, craving Maggi. You don’t just throw everything in – you follow steps: boil water → add masala → wait 2 min. That’s an algorithm! Today you learn how computers think exactly like you make Maggi – step by step. Your old lazy self ends here. Welcome to your new coder identity.”
Step 2: Curated YouTube (15 min)
Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ebe9NFgQnnU (Apna College – “How to Start Coding? Learn Programming for Beginners | 2026”)
Focus Note: “Watch only the first 12 minutes. Pay attention to ‘algorithm = real-life steps’. Pause and think: how would I explain this to my mom?”

“Algorithm = clear steps to solve any problem. Pseudocode = English steps before real code. Example: Maggi algorithm → boil → add → stir. Real-life exercise: Write steps to ‘search your crush’s name on Instagram’.”
